# 📊 Amazon Sales Analytics Dashboard (2025)

A Power BI dashboard project analyzing Amazon product sales, pricing strategies, discount effectiveness, and customer engagement trends. The report provides actionable insights to support data-driven business decisions related to product performance, pricing optimization, and category growth.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

This project leverages Amazon sales and product review data from 2025 to build an interactive Business Intelligence solution in Power BI.

The dashboard is divided into three analytical views:

- Executive Overview
- Pricing & Discount Analysis
- Product Performance Analysis

The objective is to help stakeholders understand customer behavior, identify top-performing products, evaluate discount strategies, and optimize category-level performance.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

Amazon manages thousands of products across multiple categories, each with different pricing structures, discount levels, and customer engagement metrics.

Business stakeholders need answers to critical questions such as:

- Which categories generate the highest customer engagement?
- Are discounts influencing customer satisfaction?
- Which products drive the most reviews and ratings?
- Which categories deserve more marketing investment?
- Are current discounting strategies sustainable?

Without a centralized analytics solution, it becomes difficult to monitor performance and make informed business decisions.

This dashboard addresses these challenges through interactive visualizations and KPI tracking.

# 📂 Dataset Information

The dataset contains Amazon product sales and review information from 2025.

### Dataset Features

| Column | Description |
|----------|----------|
| Transaction Date | Date of transaction |
| Product ID | Unique product identifier |
| Product Name | Product title |
| Group Category | Main category |
| Category | Sub-category |
| Actual Price | Original selling price |
| Discounted Price | Price after discount |
| Discount Percentage | Applied discount rate |
| Rating | Product rating score |
| Rating Count | Total customer reviews |

# 📈 Key Findings

## 1. Electronics Dominates Customer Engagement

Electronics generated approximately **15.8 million ratings**, making it the most engaging category.

| Category | Total Ratings |
|----------|----------|
| Electronics | 15.8M |
| Computers & Accessories | 7.7M |
| Home & Kitchen | 3.0M |

### Business Impact

Technology-related products account for the majority of customer interactions and marketplace visibility.

---

## 2. Heavy Discounting Strategy

Average discount across all products:

**47.69%**

### Business Impact

Discounting remains a major driver of competitiveness across Amazon categories.

---

## 3. Electronics Represents the Largest Revenue Opportunity

The category contributes the highest aggregated value for:

- Actual Price
- Discounted Price

### Business Impact

Electronics remains Amazon's strongest category despite significant discounting.

---

## 4. Discounts Have Weak Correlation With Ratings

Products receiving larger discounts do not consistently achieve higher customer ratings.

### Business Impact

Customer satisfaction appears more dependent on product quality than promotional pricing.

---

## 5. Customer Reviews Are Highly Concentrated

Top-performing products generated:

- Up to 850K reviews
- Multiple products above 300K reviews

### Business Impact

A small subset of products drives a significant portion of customer engagement.

---

## 6. Home & Kitchen Shows Growth Potential

Home & Kitchen products maintain:

- Strong ratings
- Competitive discounts
- Consistent engagement

### Business Impact

This category presents opportunities for expansion and targeted promotional campaigns.
